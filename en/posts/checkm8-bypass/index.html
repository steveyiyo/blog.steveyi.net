Sometimes, due to certain reasons, when an iPhone is reset or updated without turning off “Find My”, you will be asked for the Apple ID password to disable activation lock when resetting it. If you forget the Apple ID password, the iPhone can only be used as a spare part or a useless brick. Therefore, we may need to bypass this lock so that we can still use it as a game console or a music player.

First, you need to use checkra1n to jailbreak your device. Go to [https://checkra.in/](the official website) and click on the red box.

Then drag the jailbreak software checkra1n to the Applications folder, and you will find it in the Launchpad, but you don’t need to use it yet.

Next, go to the official website of checkm8.info and click on the orange “Download Free Version” button to download the software. Follow the steps to install it, then close it and open checkra1n to jailbreak your device.

Go to the Launchpad, open the checkm8.info Software [FREE VERSION] that you just installed, and connect the jailbroken device. Stay on the screen where the activation lock requires you to enter the password, and click on Start to see the magic happen. After some basic settings, you can use the device.
